I am a Computer Science student at Keshav Memorial Institute of Technology, and I am an aspiring student with a passion for technology and problem-solving. I am always seeking out new opportunities to learn and grow as a developer.

This website, "Find My Friend", is a platform that allows users to search for and view images of their friends or classmates by entering their roll number. The images are retrieved from a public database, and users can download the images if they wish. The website is designed to be user-friendly and responsive, so it can be accessed from any device.
